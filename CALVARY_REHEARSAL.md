# Calvary Hephzibah — Beamcue Rehearsal Script

**Service date:** Sunday 31 May 2026
**Use:** Sample slide content to push through Beamcue during rehearsal mode.
Open `/dev/calvary` on one screen, `/overlay/calvary-hephzibah` on another, and work through the script below tapping each slide.

The point of this rehearsal is NOT to look at the slides — it's to feel:
- Does the slide-type picker UX work for a flustered Sunday operator?
- Does the staged-then-push pattern make sense in real flow?
- Does the overlay rendering look right with Calvary's red?
- What's missing that would frustrate a real operator?

## What to watch for during rehearsal

Mark anything that frustrates you. The honest list of likely issues:

- [ ] Did the scripture text wrap properly on the overlay?
- [ ] Is the Calvary red colour rendering correctly?
- [ ] Is the staged → push pattern frictionless, or do you keep forgetting to push?
- [ ] Did the "Cut to black" feel like a one-tap action or did you hesitate?
- [ ] What's missing? (E.g. you'd want a "song lyrics" type for the next verse coming up. Or a "countdown timer.")
- [ ] How small is the operator app text on your actual phone?
- [ ] Does the realtime sync feel instant, or is there a noticeable lag?

Write down what you learn. That list IS the v1.1 backlog.
