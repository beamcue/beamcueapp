FAQ

The questions we get asked.

Does it work with OBS Studio?

Yes. Beamcue is designed as an OBS Browser Source. If you already use OBS, you're 60 seconds from being set up.

What about vMix, Streamlabs, or Wirecast?

Anything that supports a Browser Source URL works. We test against OBS first because it's free and the most common in church AV.

Do I need a Mac?

No. Beamcue runs entirely in the browser. The operator app works on any phone, tablet, or computer. OBS itself runs on Mac, Windows, or Linux.

Can my whole team push slides?

Yes. Invite team members from your dashboard. Worship leader on stage, media operator at the back — both can push at the same time without conflicts.

What Bible translations are included?

KJV (King James) is bundled. For other translations, paste verse text directly. ESV and NIV support is on the roadmap.

What if my internet drops mid-service?

The current slide stays on the overlay. Your operator app reconnects automatically. The overlay never goes blank unless you tell it to.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your dashboard. No phone calls, no retention email cycle.
